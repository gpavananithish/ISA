About the Indian Space Agency

The Indian Space Agency (ISA) is the premier space exploration agency of India. Established with the vision to harness space technology for national development, ISA has evolved into one of the leading space agencies in the world. We are dedicated to exploring the vast unknown, pursuing space science research, and developing cutting-edge technologies for the benefit of humanity.

Our Vision

To harness space technology for national development while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration. We aim to be a global leader in space technology, fostering self-reliance and international cooperation.

Our Legacy

From the launch of our first satellite, Aryabhata, in 1975, to the historic Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan missions, ISA has consistently demonstrated its capability to undertake complex space missions with cost-effectiveness and precision. Our journey is a testament to the dedication and brilliance of our scientists and engineers.
