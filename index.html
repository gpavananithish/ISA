Gateway to the Cosmos

The Indian Space Agency (ISA) is the pioneer space exploration agency of the Government of India. Our vision is to harness space technology for national development, while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration. From launching satellites that connect our nation to exploring the mysteries of the Moon and Mars, ISA is committed to excellence in space science.
